Who We Are

The leaders behind gamedays at Ross-Ade

The Ross-Ade Brigade is the official student section of Purdue Boilermakers football, driven by the energy and tradition of Ross-Ade Stadium.

Our members lead the game-day atmosphere through chants, traditions, and student engagement that define Purdue football.

This site is maintained by alumni and student leaders to connect past, present, and future members of the Brigade.
